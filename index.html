About Me

I am a B.Tech Computer Science graduate with a strong foundation in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Science, and Generative AI. I enjoy building end-to-end AI applications that solve real-world problems by combining data, machine learning models, and modern AI frameworks. I have developed projects across Machine Learning, Computer Vision, NLP, Data Analytics, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). My recent work includes a PDF Chatbot built using LangChain, ChromaDB, HuggingFace Embeddings, Google Gemini, and Streamlit, along with projects such as Chat With Website(RAG base), Rent Price Prediction, Fake Job Detection, Facial Emotion Recognition, Customer Segmentation, IPL Analysis, and Road Accident Analysis. My technical skills include Python, SQL, LangChain, TensorFlow, Keras, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, Pandas, NumPy, OpenCV, Flask, Streamlit, and ChromaDB. I have hands-on experience with data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, feature engineering, model training, transfer learning, semantic search, prompt engineering, vector databases, and deploying AI-powered applications. I am passionate about continuously learning emerging AI technologies and applying them to build practical, scalable, and impactful solutions.
